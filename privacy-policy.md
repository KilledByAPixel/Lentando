# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 8, 2026

## 1. Who we are

- **App name:** Lentando
- **Website:** https://killedbyapixel.github.io/Lentando
- **Contact:** frankforce@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how **Lentando** collects, uses, and shares information when you use the app.

## 2. What data we collect

Depending on how you sign in and use the app, we may collect:

### A) Account and authentication data (via Firebase Authentication)

- If you sign in with Google, we may receive basic profile info such as your Google account **email address**, **display name**, and **profile photo URL** (if available).
- We also store a Firebase **UID** (unique user identifier) to keep your account working.

### B) App data you provide

- We store the information you enter into the app, such as usage entries (including timestamps), to-do items, and settings.
- This data is stored in **Cloud Firestore** and associated with your Firebase user ID (UID).

### C) Technical data

Basic device and usage information that may be collected by Firebase (for example **IP address**, **user agent**, **timestamps**) to operate and secure the service.

### D) Analytics

We use Google Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand how the app is used and to improve performance.

Analytics may automatically collect information such as:
- Usage and session statistics
- Approximate location (derived from network information)
- Browser/device information
- Pages visited and basic interaction events (for example page views)

We do not intentionally send Google Analytics information that directly identifies you.

## 3. How we use data

We use your information to:
- Provide sign-in and account features
- Store and sync your app data across devices
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues
- Improve app reliability and performance

## 4. How we share data

We do **not** sell your personal information.

We share data only:
- With service providers used to run the app, primarily **Google Firebase** (Authentication, database, hosting, etc.)
- If required by law or to protect rights and safety
- With your consent (for example, if you choose to export or share content)

## 5. Google OAuth and limited use disclosure

If you sign in with Google, our use of information received from Google APIs will comply with the **Google API Services User Data Policy**, including the **Limited Use** requirements.

## 6. Data storage and retention

- Your data is stored in Firebase in the project operated by **Frank Force Games**.
- We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.
- You can request deletion (see "Your choices" below).

## 7. Your choices and controls

- **Access / update:** You can view and update some information within the app.
- **Delete your data:** You can request deletion by contacting us at **frankforce@gmail.com** with the email you used to sign in. We will delete your user data within a reasonable period, unless we must keep it for legal or security reasons.
- **Disconnect Google access:** You can remove the app’s access from your Google Account settings.

## 8. Children’s privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable safeguards (including Firebase security rules) to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 10. International users

Your information may be processed in countries where Google or our service providers operate, which may include the United States.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date and, if changes are significant, provide a notice in the app or on the website.
