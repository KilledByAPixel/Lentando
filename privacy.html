Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lentando ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the App. Lentando is designed to work without an account (local-only) or with an account (cloud sync). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are

  • App name: Lentando
  • Website: lentando.3d2k.com
  • Contact: frankforce@gmail.com
  • Operator: Frank Force Games

2. What data we collect

What data exists depends on how you use Lentando:

A) No-account mode (local-only)

  • You can use Lentando without creating an account.
  • In this mode, your data is stored on your device in browser local storage (for example: events, settings, to-dos, theme, badges, and login-skip state).
  • In no-account mode, we do not store your habit/event content in our cloud database.

B) Account and authentication data (if you sign in)

  • If you sign in with Google, Firebase Authentication may provide your email address, display name, and profile photo URL (if available).
  • If you sign in with email/password, Firebase Authentication stores your authentication credentials and account email.
  • We use a Firebase UID (unique user identifier) so your cloud data is associated with your account.

C) App data (if cloud sync is enabled)

  • If you sign in, Lentando syncs your app data to Cloud Firestore for backup and multi-device use.
  • This can include entries/events (with timestamps), settings, to-do items, badges/progress metadata, and sync metadata needed to merge changes.
  • This cloud data is associated with your Firebase UID.

D) Technical/operational data

When you use Firebase-backed features, Google/Firebase may process technical data such as IP address, device/browser information, and timestamps to provide and secure those services.

E) Browser storage and session technologies

  • Lentando uses browser local storage to run core app features (including offline usage).
  • Authentication/session technologies used by Firebase are required for sign-in features.
  • Blocking required storage/session features may prevent login or sync from working correctly.

F) Analytics

As of the Last Updated date above, we do not run dedicated advertising trackers in Lentando. If we add analytics tooling in the future, we will update this policy before or when that change goes live.

3. How we use data

We use your information to:

  • Provide local-only app functionality when you choose not to sign in.
  • Provide optional sign-in, backup, and cloud sync features when you do sign in.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues.
  • Maintain and improve app reliability and performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal basis for processing (EU/UK users)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide the App and requested features (including optional account sync, if enabled by you).
  • Legitimate interests: To maintain security, stability, abuse prevention, and service operation where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent: Where required by law for specific processing activities.

If you have questions about our legal bases, contact us at frankforce@gmail.com.

5. How we share data

We do not sell your personal information.

We share data only:

  • With service providers used to run the App, primarily Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore cloud database).
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Frank Force Games, our users, or others.
  • With your consent (for example, if you choose to export or share content).

6. Google sign-in disclosure

If you choose Google sign-in, your authentication data is processed through Firebase Authentication and Google services. We use that data only for account access and app operation, not for selling personal data or unrelated advertising purposes.

7. Data storage and retention

  • Local-only mode: Data remains on your device until you delete it in-app, clear browser storage, or uninstall browser/app data.
  • Signed-in mode: Synced data is stored in Firebase under your account (UID).
  • You can delete cloud account data using the in-app delete account flow. You can also clear local app data in-app.
  • We may retain limited records where required for legal, security, or compliance obligations.

8. Your choices and controls

  • Use without account: You can continue without account sign-in and use local-only mode.
  • Access / Update: You can view and update app data directly within the App.
  • Clear all app data: The App includes an in-app control to clear local data. If you are signed in, this flow also attempts to clear your synced cloud data.
  • Delete account and cloud data: If signed in, you can delete your account and associated cloud data from within the App settings.
  • Disconnect Google access: You can remove the App's access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account settings (Security > Third-party apps with account access).
  • Support requests: You can also contact us at frankforce@gmail.com for privacy requests.

EU/UK users additionally have the right to: access a copy of your personal data, correct inaccurate data, object to or restrict certain processing, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. California users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, or sell.
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to certain exceptions).
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at frankforce@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

10. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at frankforce@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.
